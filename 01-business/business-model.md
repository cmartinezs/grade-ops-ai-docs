# Business Model

## Customer
Small education operators teaching programming: bootcamps, tutors, cohort-based academies, and instructor-led training teams.

## Offering
A workflow product that reduces assessment operations time while improving consistency and reporting.

## Revenue model
- Monthly subscription by educator or small team
- Higher tier for institutions managing multiple cohorts
- Optional setup or pilot package for onboarding support

## Why this can work
The buyer already experiences grading workload as a recurring operational cost. GradeOps AI aims to convert saved time and improved learner support into a clear software spend.
