# Pricing

## Principles
- Simple enough to explain in a hackathon demo
- Low-friction for early adopters
- High enough to validate business value

## Draft pricing
- Solo educator: $29-49/month
- Small team: $99-199/month
- Pilot package: custom pricing for short guided onboarding

## Early validation goals
- Test whether educators will pay for grading and feedback operations support first
- Learn whether pricing should follow seats, cohorts, or assessment volume
