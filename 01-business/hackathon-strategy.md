# Hackathon Strategy

## Objective
Present GradeOps AI as a viable business with a focused MVP and early market evidence.

## What to demonstrate
- End-to-end assessment workflow for programming education
- Distinct agent roles with teacher control points
- Logs showing real agent operations
- Evidence of user conversations, usage, and commercial intent

## Deliverables to prioritize
- Clear positioning and pitch
- Demonstrable MVP workflow
- Customer discovery notes
- Usage and revenue evidence templates
- A credible next-step pilot plan
