# Go-to-Market

## Initial wedge
Programming educators with recurring cohort assessments and limited operational support.

## Entry motion
- Founder-led outreach to bootcamps, tutors, and academies
- Live demo using a real assessment workflow
- Short pilot focused on one assignment or cohort

## Early channels
- Existing educator networks
- Coding bootcamp communities
- Tutor and academy referrals
- Hackathon exposure and follow-up conversations

## Sales message
GradeOps AI is not a quiz generator. It is an assessment operations layer that helps teachers keep control while AI handles repetitive workflow steps.
