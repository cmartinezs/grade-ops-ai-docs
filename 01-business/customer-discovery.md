# Customer Discovery

## Key questions
- Where does assessment work consume the most instructor time?
- Which steps are painful enough to pay for?
- What review controls are required for trust?
- What proof is needed before an educator will adopt AI support?

## Interview targets
- Independent coding tutors
- Bootcamp instructors
- Small academy founders
- Program managers overseeing grading consistency

## Evidence to capture
- Interview date and role
- Current workflow and tools
- Pain severity
- Buying trigger
- Objections
- Willingness to pilot or pay
