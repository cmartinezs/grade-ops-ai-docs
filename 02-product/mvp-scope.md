# MVP Scope

## In scope
- One programming assessment workflow
- Assessment brief intake
- Rubric generation draft
- Submission tracking
- AI grading assistance with teacher review
- Feedback draft generation
- Learning gap summary
- Recovery activity suggestion
- Teacher report generation
- Agent log and usage evidence capture

## Out of scope
- Full LMS replacement
- Broad non-programming subject support
- Fully autonomous grading without human approval
- Enterprise integrations beyond simple exports or APIs
