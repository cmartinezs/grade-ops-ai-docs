# Vision

GradeOps AI helps programming educators run assessment operations with AI agents while preserving teacher control.

## Long-term goal
Become the operating layer for assessment workflows in small and mid-sized programming education environments.

## What success looks like
- Teachers spend less time on repetitive assessment work
- Feedback quality becomes more consistent across cohorts
- Learning gaps are detected earlier
- Small education teams can operate with less manual overhead
- The business collects clear evidence of value and willingness to pay
